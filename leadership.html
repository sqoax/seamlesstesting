Leadership

Leadership grounded in healthcare, service, and experience.

Seamless Point of Care is led by hands-on operators with decades of experience in healthcare I.T., low-voltage cabling, and point-of-care integration.

Tim Gafnea

Founder / Managing Partner

In 1994, Tim founded what is now Seamless Point of Care, a division of IRG of Georgia, LLC. Starting as a humble low-voltage cabling company, it grew into a dental technology integration firm with over 250 dental clients. In 2005, Tim sold his dental client base to focus on the acute and ambulatory healthcare I.T. market. Today, Seamless POC is one of the leading healthcare I.T. integration companies in the nation.

As the managing partner, Tim’s responsibilities include development and execution of corporate strategy and cultivating strategic partnerships with clients and vendors. He is committed to providing the very best working environment and relationships for the Seamless POC team of employees and the clients they serve.

He currently serves on Ingram Micro’s Healthcare IT & Pro A/V Advisory Councils and is a frequent lecturer on healthcare I.T. point-of-care challenges and solutions.

Tim is a U.S. Navy veteran and lives in Brevard, NC with his wife Corey and their two children. He enjoys fishing, canoeing, mountain biking, guitar pickin’, and mountain living.

Jim Cole

Director of Sales / Co-owner

Jim Cole is the co-owner and Director of Sales for Seamless POC. Before joining the Seamless team in 2006, Jim spent more than 22 years providing communication systems to the medical community.

Jim leads the Seamless sales force by example, providing his own client base with exemplary service, experience, and attention to detail. He is a true point-of-care pro with hundreds of projects and successful rollouts under his belt.

A native of California and U.S. Navy veteran, Jim has been a resident of the Southeastern United States for over 25 years. He and his wife Julien share their historic home in Jefferson, Georgia with their dogs Lucy, Tater, and a host of free-range cats.
