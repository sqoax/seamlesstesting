How our IT Ninjas work

Designed for clinical spaces, delivered with minimal disruption.

Hospitals are not construction sites. We plan installs around patient care, infection control, and privacy, then leave rooms cleaner than we found them.

1. Evaluate and design

Walkthroughs, measurements, and conversations with IS and clinical leaders to understand workflows, constraints, and goals.

  • Room by room assessment and elevation plans.
  • Hardware neutral recommendations that respect your standards.

2. Install and clean up

Quiet, respectful work in sensitive areas. We protect surfaces, control dust, and haul away debris when we are done.

  • Evening or weekend work windows when you need them.
  • Rooms returned in clinical ready condition.

3. Support and lifecycle

After go live, we stay available for adjustments, repairs, and refreshes so layouts keep working as your care models evolve.

  • Maintenance visits and service calls.
  • Planning for new units and future expansion.
